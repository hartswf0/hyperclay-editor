Papers (Content Examples)
Position Papers
Module 2 — Position Paper PP1

Research Innis and McLuhan on oral vs. print culture; choose a topic and argue merits/limits.

Rubric: Substance; Structure; Clarity; Analysis; Evidence; Originality; Engagement.

Module 3 — Newspaper Position Paper PP2

Create a newspaper’s main idea & editorial stance; include a Letter to the Editor reflecting alignment.

Rubric: Viewpoint; Editorial Stance; Editor Spotlight; Position.

Radio Broadcast Composer / Language Game Creator PP3

Develop a radio script integrating linguistic, sociological, and philosophical theories, or design a language game.

Rubric: Analysis; Concept; Defense; Synthesis; Components; Evaluation.

Ownership & Ethics of AI‑Generated Content PP4

Analyze ownership of AI‑generated content; audit data flows; argue using Lessig’s frameworks.

Rubric: Lessig Analysis; Claim via GenAI; Data Audit; Philosophy of Ownership.

Comment Papers
Module 2 — Comment Paper CP1

Peer review two position papers; be analytic, comparative, and constructive.

Guidelines: Specific; Examples; Balanced; Respectful; Objective.

Module 3 — Newspaper Comment Paper CP2

Write a Letter to the Editor evaluating an article against the newspaper’s stance.

Guidelines: Word Limit; Structure; Coherence; Clarity; Critical Analysis; Constructive Feedback.
